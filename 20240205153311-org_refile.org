#+title: org-refile
#+filetags: :report:text:editing:move:section:org-refile:org-mode:

To move one section into another section in org-mode is really easy. We can use
org-refile (default kbd: C-c C-w) by putting out cursor at the section we want
to move and calling org-refile, then select the section you want to move it to
when prompted in the minibuffer.
