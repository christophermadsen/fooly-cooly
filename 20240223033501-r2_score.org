#+title: r2-score
#+filetags: :evaluation:ml:machine learning:metric:

A simple explanation of the /r2-score/: It's a metric which tells you *how close*
your prediction is to the truth. Also called the /coefficient of
determination/. The best possible score is *1.0*, which are perfect predictions,
where *0.0* are all imperfect predictions.
